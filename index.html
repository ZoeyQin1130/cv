Zihan Qin

Chinese Lecturer at Washington University in St. Louis. I like to play tennis and hike with my dog.

Zihan Qin is a Chinese lectuere at Washington University in St. Louis. She got her master's degree in second language acquisition at the University of Iowa. She has extensive experiences in teaching beginners to advanced learners as well as heritage speakers. She has taught at different intensive summer programs, including Duke Study in China, Princeton in Beijing, and Middlebulry Chinese School. Her focus is improve students all four skills in listening, speaking, writing and reading utilizing authentic and multimedia materials. Her reseraches focus on heritage instruction, advanced reading and writing, multimedia in language instruction and bilingualism.
